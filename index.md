# ScanFlow – Document Scanner

**The smartest way to scan, edit, sign, and manage documents on your device.**

ScanFlow turns your phone into a powerful document workstation. Scan documents with your camera, edit PDFs, add signatures, extract text with OCR, and manage QR codes — all privately, all offline, all on your device.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📄 Document Scanner
Capture crisp, high-quality scans of any document with your device camera. Auto-detect edges, correct perspective, and enhance readability — all in one tap.

### ✏️ PDF Editor
Edit your PDFs directly on your device. Add text, annotate, rearrange pages, and make changes without needing a desktop computer.

### ✍️ PDF Sign
Sign documents digitally right from your phone. Create and save your signature, then apply it to any PDF instantly — no printing required.

### 🖍️ Highlight & Annotate
Mark up your PDFs with highlights, underlines, and annotations. Emphasize key sections and make your documents work for you.

### 🔍 PDF to OCR
Extract text from any PDF using on-device OCR. Turn scanned documents and images into editable, searchable text — fast and private.

### 📷 Image to OCR
Snap a photo of any text and instantly extract editable content. Works with printed text, handwritten notes, signs, and more.

### 📱 QR Code Scanner
Scan any QR code instantly with your camera. Open links, save contacts, connect to Wi-Fi, and more — all in a fraction of a second.

### 🔗 QR Code Generator
Create custom QR codes for URLs, text, contacts, Wi-Fi credentials, and more. Generate, customize, and share QR codes in seconds.
