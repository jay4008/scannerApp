ScanFlow Features

Everything you need to scan, edit, sign, and manage documents — all in one app.

📄

Document Scanner

Turn your phone into a professional-grade document scanner.

  • Auto edge detection
  • Perspective correction
  • Enhancement filters
  • Multi-page support
  • All on-device
✏️

PDF Editor

Edit PDFs without needing a computer.

  • Add & edit text directly
  • Rearrange pages with drag-and-drop
  • Annotate and markup with drawing tools
  • Insert images into existing PDFs
  • Save changes instantly on device
✍️

PDF Sign

Sign documents digitally — no printing, no scanning, no hassle.

  • Create your signature with finger or stylus
  • Save multiple signatures
  • Apply signatures with one tap
  • Add dates and initials
  • Legally sign from anywhere
🖍️

Highlight & Annotate

Mark up your PDFs to emphasize what matters.

  • Highlight text in multiple colors
  • Underline and strikethrough
  • Add text annotations & sticky notes
  • Draw freehand on any page
  • Organize annotations by color or type
🔍

PDF to OCR

Extract text from any PDF — turn scans into editable content.

  • On-device OCR — no internet required
  • Accurate text recognition
  • Searchable output
  • Copy extracted text to clipboard
  • Multi-language support
📷

Image to OCR

Snap a photo and extract text instantly.

  • Camera-based scanning
  • Works with printed text & handwritten notes
  • Real-time processing
  • Save or share extracted text
  • 100% offline — no cloud
📱

QR Code Scanner

Scan any QR code in an instant.

  • Instant camera scan
  • Open URLs directly
  • Save contacts from vCard codes
  • Connect to Wi-Fi from QR codes
  • View all QR types
🔗

QR Code Generator

Create custom QR codes for anything.

  • URL QR codes — share links instantly
  • Text QR codes — encode any message
  • Contact QR codes — share vCards
  • Wi-Fi QR codes — let others connect
  • Customize colors & export as image
