# ScanFlow Features

**Everything you need to scan, edit, sign, and manage documents — all in one app.**

---

## 📄 Document Scanner

Turn your phone into a professional-grade document scanner.

- **Auto edge detection** — ScanFlow automatically finds document edges
- **Perspective correction** — Straightens tilted scans instantly
- **Enhancement filters** — Improve clarity, contrast, and readability
- **Multi-page support** — Scan multiple pages into one document
- **All on-device** — Your scans never leave your phone

---

## ✏️ PDF Editor

Edit PDFs without needing a computer.

- **Add & edit text** directly in your PDF
- **Rearrange pages** with drag-and-drop ease
- **Annotate and markup** with drawing tools
- **Insert images** into existing PDFs
- **Save changes** instantly on your device

---

## ✍️ PDF Sign

Sign documents digitally — no printing, no scanning, no hassle.

- **Create your signature** with your finger or stylus
- **Save multiple signatures** for different uses
- **Apply signatures** to any page with one tap
- **Add dates and initials** alongside your signature
- **Legally sign** documents from anywhere

---

## 🖍️ Highlight & Annotate

Mark up your PDFs to emphasize what matters.

- **Highlight text** in multiple colors
- **Underline and strikethrough** for edits
- **Add text annotations** and sticky notes
- **Draw freehand** on any page
- **Organize annotations** by color or type

---

## 🔍 PDF to OCR

Extract text from any PDF — turn scans into editable content.

- **On-device OCR** — no internet required, fully private
- **Accurate text recognition** across fonts and layouts
- **Searchable output** — find any word in your documents
- **Copy extracted text** to clipboard or save as a file
- **Multi-language support** for global use

---

## 📷 Image to OCR

Snap a photo and extract the text instantly.

- **Camera-based scanning** — point, shoot, extract
- **Works with printed text**, handwritten notes, signs, labels
- **Real-time processing** — results in seconds
- **Save extracted text** as a document or share directly
- **100% offline** — no cloud, no data leaks

---

## 📱 QR Code Scanner

Scan any QR code in an instant.

- **Instant camera scan** — point and scan, that's it
- **Open URLs** directly from scanned codes
- **Save contacts** from vCard QR codes
- **Connect to Wi-Fi** from network QR codes
- **View all QR types** — text, URL, email, phone, SMS, and more

---

## 🔗 QR Code Generator

Create custom QR codes for anything.

- **URL QR codes** — share links instantly
- **Text QR codes** — encode any message
- **Contact QR codes** — share vCards
- **Wi-Fi QR codes** — let others connect with a scan
- **Customize colors** and export as image
