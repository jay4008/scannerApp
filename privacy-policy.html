Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: 21 Sept 2025

ScanFlow ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, ScanFlow – Document Scanner.

1. Information We Collect

When you use ScanFlow, we may collect the following information:

Category Details
Device Information Your device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
App Usage Data Features you use within the app, crash reports, and performance data.
Optional Data If you choose to provide your email or other information for support or feedback.
Note: ScanFlow does not collect personal documents, images, or text content from your device. All scanning and processing are done locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app.
  • To diagnose technical issues and fix bugs.
  • To understand user behavior for improving app performance and features.
  • To provide support if you contact us.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share anonymized, aggregated usage data with service providers for analytics purposes.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

ScanFlow may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, or advertising. These third parties may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. Examples include:

  • Google Firebase — analytics and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — ads

6. Your Choices

You can control what information the app can access:

  • Permissions: You can allow or deny access to camera, storage, or other device features.
  • Data Sharing: You can choose not to provide optional information when contacting support.

7. Children's Privacy

ScanFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend checking this page periodically for any updates.

Your continued use of ScanFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
